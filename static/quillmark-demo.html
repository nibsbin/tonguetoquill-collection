QuillMark Syntax Highlighting

This demonstrates the QuillMark syntax highlighting system implemented in CodeMirror 6. The highlighting includes:

  • Metadata blocks - Visually distinct with background tint and left border
  • SCOPE/QUILL keywords - Highlighted in USAF blue
  • Identifiers - Scope and quill names in cyan
  • YAML content - Keys, strings, numbers, and booleans with type-specific colors
  • Horizontal rule detection - Distinguishes metadata delimiters from HR
--- QUILL: taro author: Nibs ice_cream: Taro title: 'My Favorite Ice Cream Flavor' count: 42 enabled: true --- I love Taro ice cream for its subtly sweet, nutty flavor and creamy texture. --- SCOPE: quotes author: Albert Einstein --- Without taro ice cream, life would be a mistake.
